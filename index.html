Biography

Indian politician Narendra Modi was born on September 17, 1950, in Vadnagar. He advanced through the ranks of the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS) and Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP). From 2001 to 2014, Modi presided as Gujarat's chief minister. He was appointed as India's 14th prime minister in 2014. Under his leadership, the emphasis has been placed on foreign policy endeavours, infrastructure development, and economic reforms. The "Make in India" initiative, which sought to increase domestic manufacturing, and Narendra Modi's strong leadership style have made him a divisive figure in Indian politics. During his administration, the economic expansion has received both praise and condemnation for its contribution to social and religious issues.
