Biography

Indian politician Narendra Modi, born on September 17, 1950, in Vadnagar, is the 14th prime minister of India. From 2001 to 2014, Modi served as Gujarat's chief minister. His leadership emphasizes foreign policy, infrastructure development, and economic reforms. The "Make in India" initiative aimed to boost domestic manufacturing. Modi's strong leadership style has made him a divisive figure in Indian politics.
